Biography

Steve Jobs (February 24,1955-Octber 5,2011) was an American enterpreneur , industrial designer , businnes magnate, medea proprietor and an investor. He as the co-founder , chairman and CEO of Apple;the chairman and majority shareholdr of Pixar;a member of the Walt Disney Company's board of directors following its acquisition of Pixar ;and the founder, chairman and CEO of NeXT.He was widely recognized as a pioneer of the personal computer revolution of the 1970s and 1980s , along with his early business partner and fellow Apple co- founder Steve Wozniak.
